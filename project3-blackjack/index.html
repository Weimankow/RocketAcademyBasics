♠ ♥ Blackjack Game ♦ ♣


Win or Lose Conditions

You will lose immediately when your card totals more than 21 points. That's called a 'bust'.

You will win immediately if you get exactly 21 points.

You can also win if at the end of the game, your points are higher than the dealer's points, but not more than 21 points.

How to Play

When the game starts, the dealer will deal two cards to you, and two cards to himself.

It will then be your turn to decide any actions you want to take, then the dealer's turn, and then the game will end.

If you want to take another card, select 'Hit'

If you want to pass, select 'Stand'

You can 'Hit' as many times as you want within your round, but the moment you 'Stand', it will become the dealer's turn.

How to Calculate Card Points

Any face card (jack, queen, king) = 10 points

Any number card will be equals to their number value.

For example, 8 of hearts = 8 points, ace of spades = 1 point
