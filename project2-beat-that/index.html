Beat That Basic Version!

Hello! Welcome to Beat That!

This is a two player game. Player 1 will start first.

Step 1: Click 'Submit' to roll 2 dice.
Step 2: Think about which dice roll should come first to create the largest number.
Step 3: If you want dice 1's number to come first, Type '1', otherwise, type '2'.
It will then be the second player's turn.

The player with the highest number wins! Good luck!

Player 1's turn - Press Submit to roll your dice!
